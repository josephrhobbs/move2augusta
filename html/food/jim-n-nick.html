Review

I had the opportunity to go to Jim 'N Nick's BBQ recently with two of my friends. The staff were very welcoming and polite, and the barbecue was very good. The meat was well-cooked and high quality. I had coleslaw with my BBQ, which was also quite good.

As we arrived later in the evening, the staff also generously offered us corn muffins with cheese and banana or coconut pudding as they were closing. The corn muffins and pudding were both very good. I definitely did not leave hungry. I'm not a barbecue connoisseur, but I would recommend it!
