Home Apartments Contributing

Are you moving to Augusta, GA? Have you been living in Augusta for a while now? Would you like to see an apartment complex, your favorite restaurant, or a fun activity represented here?

We would love to include your suggestions and contributions in the Move2Augusta guide. Please contact the Editor at editor@move2augusta.com with your suggestion. If you would like, you can even provide a website link, photos that you've taken, or special tips. You can also let us know if you'd like your contribution to be in your name, or as an anonymous reader.
