# Contributing

~

Are you moving to Augusta, GA?  Have you been living in Augusta for a while now?  _Would you like to see an apartment complex, your favorite restaurant, or a fun activity represented here?_

We would love to include your suggestions and contributions in the _Move2Augusta_ guide.  Please contact the Editor at [editor@move2augusta.com](mailto:editor@move2augusta.com) with your suggestion.  If you would like, you can even provide a website link, photos that you've taken, or special tips.  You can also let us know if you'd like your contribution to be in your name, or as an anonymous reader.
